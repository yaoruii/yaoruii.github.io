About me

EE TO CS/DA/ML. Focusing!

Hi! This is Ruiyao!

I am a undergrauate student from DaLian University of Technology in DaLian, Liaoning, China, majoring in Electronic Information Engineering from Sep 2015 to Jun 2019.

Now I am taking a gap year befor beginning my graduate education because I didn't figure out what I want and where I want to spend 1-2 years on my graduate education last year. So I didn't apply for any graduate programs overseas or in China. Besides, I want to enhance my background and experience many different things during this free year.

For now, I am a RA in Research Center for Ubiquitous Computing System, Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. My supervisor is Zhiwen Pan.

The research areas are data mining, anomaly detection, deep learning and meachine learning. During my undergrauate study, I spent half year on face attributes manipulation in the Computer Vision Lab in DLUT.
